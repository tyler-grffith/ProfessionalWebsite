My Statement of Purpose

As an Aerospace Engineer

I am interested in substance, form, and movement. Reaction to stimuli. How do fundamental situations evolve and how to quantify the variations that real-world effects have on those platonic ideals? In constantly pursuing those curiosities, I aim to shed light on the boundaries of what our vehicles and physical machines can do.

My goal is to organize information. To consolidate what I have learned and will continue to learn into a referenceable, interactive, and adaptive aerospace/physics focused calculator. A versatile UI that draws on the documented libraries of my notes, equations, and style to read my prompts, display the fully defined and the degrees of freedom of the situation, and create figures of the results to my specifications.

Think Wolfram Alpha for gas dynamics, orbital mechanics, and thermodynamic situations, but with the advantage of AI to handle less rigid requirements on input and more control over the manifestation of output.

I am inspired by the power of Desmos (Graphing & Geometry), Wolfram Alpha, SolidWorks, and spreadsheets in general. My goal is to merge their functionalities to have a quick to use, accurate, and highly adjustable omni-calculator.
